Building the Web, One Layer at a Time

I'm Govind Sharma, a Senior Software Developer and Tech Lead with over 9 years of hands-on experience in backend engineering, cloud infrastructure, and full-stack web development. My journey started with a B.Tech in Computer Science from Lovely Professional University, Punjab, and has taken me through startups, product companies, and enterprise environments.

My core strength lies in Python — Django, FastAPI, Flask — but I'm equally comfortable spinning up Angular dashboards, architecting microservices, or configuring CI/CD pipelines on AWS and Azure. I've led teams, mentored developers, and driven projects from zero to production across multiple geographies.

I thrive in fast-paced startup environments where wearing multiple hats is the norm. Whether it's designing a distributed task queue with Celery and Redis, integrating third-party payment and notification services, or optimizing a slow PostgreSQL query — I bring both technical depth and ownership to every problem.

Outside of code, I enjoy travel photography, crafting, and automating things that should've been automated years ago. I believe in continuous learning, clean commits, and writing code that future-me won't curse.
